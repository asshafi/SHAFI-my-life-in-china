Gannan Normal University

Gannan Normal University(赣南师范学院) is located in the national historical and cultural city named Ganzhou which is also a deputy central city in Jiangxi Province. The university was founded in June 1958 as a Gannan Teachers College. It was changed to Gannan Normal College in 1959 and enrolled undergraduates. It was restored to Gannan Teachers College in 1964. The name of the university has changed many times since then. In 1984, it was restored to Gannan Normal College and upgraded to a provincial undergraduate normal college. It became a master’s degree awarding unit in 2003 which is the border between the four provinces of Jiangxi, Guangdong, Fujian, and Hunan. This is the only provincial undergraduate normal university in the region with the right to grant a master’s degree. For the last time, it was renamed as Gannan Normal University with the approval of the Ministry of Education in March 2016.

Gannan Normal University(赣南师范学院) has three campuses: the Gold Campus, the Zhanggong Campus, and the Baita Campus (University Science Park), covering an area of more than 2,200 acres. The university has 15 teaching colleges (commonly known as departments), 1 independent college, 1 affiliated middle school, and 1 affiliated primary school. There are 72 undergraduate majors covering 16 disciplines including science, literature, engineering, education, management, art, law, economics, history, and agriculture. Also, There are 16 first-level master’s programs, and 9 master’s degree authorization points. There are currently more than 20,000 full-time students, including more than 1,300 graduate students. From 2006, more than 300 international students have enrolled in this university.
赣南师范学院坐落于国家历史文化名城、江西省副中心城市赣州。 学校始建于1958年6月，前身为赣南师范专科学校。 1959年改为赣南师范学院，招收本科生。 1964年恢复为赣南师范学院，此后校名多次变更。 1984年恢复赣南师范专科学校，升格为省属本科师范院校。 2003年成为硕士学位授予单位，地处江西、广东、福建、湖南四省交界处。 这是该地区唯一一所具有硕士学位授予权的省属本科师范大学。 2016年3月，经教育部批准，学校更名为赣南师范大学。 赣南师范学院现有黄金校区、章贡校区、白塔校区（大学科技园）三个校区，占地2200余亩。 学校设有15个教学学院（俗称院系）、1个独立学院、1个附属中学、1个附属小学。 现有72个本科专业，涵盖理学、文学、工学、教育学、管理学、艺术学、法学、经济学、历史学、农学等16个学科门类。 拥有一级学科硕士学位授权点16个，硕士学位授权点9个。 现有全日制在校生2万余人，其中研究生1300余人。 自2006年以来，已有300多名国际学生在这所大学就读。 Gannan Normal University(赣南师范学院) has more than 1000 full-time teachers, including 480 senior professional and technical personnel, and more than 300 doctoral teachers. In recent years, a group of academic and academic leading talents has been introduced and cultivated. There are more than 20 academicians including dual-appointed academicians, candidates for the National New Century Multi-million Talent Project, candidates for the New Century Excellent Talents Program of the Ministry of Education, and special allowance experts from the State Council.
