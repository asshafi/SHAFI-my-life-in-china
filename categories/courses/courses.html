Gannan Normal University

Gannan Normal University(赣南师范学院) adheres to the characteristics of teacher education to promote development. The government-university cooperation teacher education system has sent more than 50,000 teachers to basic education, and is known as the “Cradle of People’s Teachers on the Red Soil of Gannan”. It is training high-quality basic education teachers and various types of senior professionals. At the same time, the university insists on being based on local and serving the grassroots, relying on various scientific research platforms and talent resources to actively serve local basic education and economic and social development.



赣南师范学院坚持教师教育特色促发展。 官校合作教师教育体系已为基础教育输送了5万多名教师，被誉为“赣南红土地上人民教师的摇篮”。 培养高素质基础教育师资和各类高级专门人才。 同时，学校坚持立足地方、服务基层，依托各类科研平台和人才资源，积极服务地方基础教育和经济社会发展。 “辅导员培训实训基地”、“江西省高校教师岗前培训基地”、“赣州市中小企业高级管理人员培训中心”等培训机构已完成“国家培训”等30余批1万人次 计划”项目培训任务。

From 2010 to 2012, more than 4300 students of Gannan Normal University(赣南师范学院) participated in various competitions above the provincial level and won awards in groups. Among them, they won the first prize of the “National Graduate (University) Mathematical Modeling Contest” and the first prize of the “National University Student Entrepreneurship Competition”. More than 180 students have won first prizes in national competitions, including the first prize for national college students’ art performances. The university has won National Civilized Units, National Legal Propaganda and Education Advanced Units, National Green Model Units, National College Mental Health Education Advanced Units, National Advanced Fitness Activities Units, National Model Workers’ Homes, National “May Fourth” Red Flag League Committee, Jiangxi Provincial Civilized Units, Jiangxi Provincial School Building Construction Civilized Units, Jiangxi Province’s first batch of “Safe & Civilized Campuses”, Jiangxi Provincial Gardening Units and other honorary titles. In November 2017, the university was awarded the first “National Civilized Campus” honorary title by the Central Steering Committee for the Construction of Spiritual Civilization.
